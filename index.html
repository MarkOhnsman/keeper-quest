Four Disciplines

⚡ Reaction Trials — A hands-free cone drill. The app calls a cone aloud; explode to it, touch it, recover to your set position. React before you think.

💪 Strength Trials — Bodyweight circuits built for goalkeeper-specific power: explosive hips, strong shoulders, lateral force.

🔥 Conditioning Gauntlet — Fitness AMRAPs (push-ups, squats, jog in place). Go max effort for the timer, then log your count and beat your record.

⚽ Ball Skills — Touch work, juggling streaks, and turn sequences. Build a first touch that never lets you down.
